Project Pemi

Planning an expedition in New Hampshire's Pemigawasset Wilderness

Overview

This microsite serves as a project plan for Nate, Ben, and Joe to successfully traverse the 31.5 miles and 10 peaks of the Pemigawasset Loop. In addition to thoroughly outlining the logistics of the trip, Project Pemi contains a number of other useful features:

  • IFTTT recipe to send Google Maps launch links to Nate's iPhone via SMS at scheduled times
  • Contigency Plan Workflow to send emails if group does not return to civilization as planned
  • Fully mobile-responsive microsite for on-the-go access
  • Realtime aggregation of Instagram photos tagged with #PemiLoop (see bottom of this page)

Advance Planning

Participants will secure all individual and group supplies specified in this spreadsheet. Joe will pick up vehicle from Hightstown, New Jersey. Participants will train to ensure physical condition will meet the trip's strenuous demands.

Saturday, May 21st

Sunday, May 22nd

7:00 AM
Break down camp. Hike ~7.5 miles to Franconia Brook Campsite via Mt. Bond and Bondcliff.

Monday, May 23rd

7:00 AM
Break down camp. Hike 3 miles to Lincoln Woods Trail Head, where vehicle is parked.

9:00 AM
Task 3 fires, sending Nate’s iPhone a Google Maps launch link with driving directions. Drive 329 miles to New York City, stopping for one or more hot meals en route. Upload photos to Instagram using #PemiLoop, so they'll appear in the gallery below!
